The Hidden Cost Crisis Every Practice Faces

"Most practices are losing $100K-$1M+ per year to chart prep inefficiency. And they don't even know it."

Time Drain

16 min/patient

Physicians spend 16 minutes per patient navigating EHRs—with 5+ minutes just on chart review. For a 20-patient day, that's 1.5 hours lost to prep work.

Lost time = 2-3 missed patient visits per day. At $150/visit, that's $300-450 in daily lost revenue per provider.

Cost Spiral

$150+/patient

Manual medical record retrieval costs $150+ per patient in most states. For a mid-sized practice, that's $30K-50K per month just getting records.

Incomplete records = missed diagnoses, lost E&M coding opportunities, unfilled care gaps = millions in lost annual revenue.

Revenue Loss

$500K-$1.5M

When providers aren't fully prepared: they code conservatively (Level 3 vs 4), miss HCC diagnoses ($3K-8K/patient), and leave care gaps open (HEDIS penalties).

For a 10-provider practice, this translates to $500K-$1.5M in preventable annual losses.
